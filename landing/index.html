What's inside

🧍
12 characters

A dev, an AI robot, a senior, a PM, a boss and one cat. 9 poses, 8 moods, gaze direction, items in hands. Repaint the outfits however you like.

💬
Comic language

Shout, thought, whisper, plus a separate robo-voice for the AI. A terminal with command chips, BAM! lettering, focus lines for drama.

📝
Script to strip

Type "hero angry: line" and get a draft strip. Then drag things around until it's actually funny.

🖱️
Everything drags

Characters, bubbles, even the tail tips move right on the panel. Missed? Cmd+Z is right there. Arrow keys for pixel work.

📤
Platform-ready export

A 1080 square for Instagram and Threads, a vertical strip for Telegram, a per-panel carousel. One button, six files.

🔒
No backend

There is no server. Strips live in localStorage, sources export as JSON. The code is open, it's all on GitHub.

🔒 We collect nothing: no analytics, no cookies, no external requests. Everything stays in your browser.
